frequently
asked questions

Do I need to install an app?

↻

No. Kommza works right in your browser — on desktop, tablet, and phone. Mobile apps for iOS and Android are also available.

Do I need to sign up?

↻

No. Guests simply click the link and join the meeting. Sign-up is only needed for advanced features like recording, webinars, and team management.

Is Kommza suitable for business?

↻

Absolutely. Team syncs, interviews, consultations, demos, and client meetings. Webinars for up to 200 people with recording.

How secure is Kommza?

↻

Dedicated local servers with full data protection compliance. Independent service — not tied to any social network or ecosystem.

Is the AI assistant available yet?

↻

AI features are rolling out in phases. The assistant will capture meeting outcomes, prepare minutes, and follow up on action items.

Can I use it for free?

↻

Yes. The Starter plan is completely free — up to 10 participants and meetings up to 1 hour. Upgrade whenever you need more.
